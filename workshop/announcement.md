Just wanted to share that some of our AI Labs engineers recently participated in the AWS AI Development Lifecycle (AI-DLC) Workshop for Partners.

The workshop introduced AI-DLC, a methodology where AI drives the planning, code generation, and testing while humans stay in control of the critical decisions. Using Kiro (both the IDE and CLI), the team went hands-on through the full lifecycle: Inception, Construction, and Operations, with development cycles measured in hours, not weeks. The approach also leans into mob programming, with rituals like Mob Elaboration, Mob Programming, and Mob Testing where Product Owners, developers, and AI all collaborate together in real time. We even shipped a Flappy Bird clone from scratch, so yes, your AI Labs team is now also a game studio. 🐦🎮

It was a great opportunity to sharpen our workflows and stay aligned with industry-leading practices. The big mindset shift? Instead of *us* prompting AI to help, the AI initiates and proposes the plan, and we approve, guide, and refine. Excited to see how we bring this "AI plans, humans decide" approach into our upcoming projects! 🚀
