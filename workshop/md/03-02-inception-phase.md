# Inception Phase

As noted at the beginning of the workshop, the first phase of AI-DLC is the Inception phase. The key activities involved in this phase focus on the Mob Elaboration ritual.

- AI asks clarifying questions (e.g., \"Who are the primary users? What key business outcomes should this achieve?\"), ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the goal.

- AI decomposes the intent into Units, e.g., \"Game Engine,\" \"Collision Detection,\" and \"Scoring System.\"

- AI elaborates each Unit into user stories, non-functional requirements (NFRs), and risk descriptions.

- The team validates these outputs, adjusting wherever needed to refine the Units. for example: the team might notice that the Game Engine Unit lacks performance requirements and adjust to include frame rate specifications.

- AI generates a PRFAQ for the Unit (optional), summarizing the business intent, functionality, and expected benefits.

- The team validates the PRFAQ and associated risks, ensuring alignment with the overall objectives.
