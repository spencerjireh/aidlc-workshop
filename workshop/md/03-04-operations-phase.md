# Operations Phase

The Operations phase transforms your working application into a production-ready system with deployment automation and intelligent monitoring. The team will achieve a fully deployed application with AI-powered observability and operational excellence.

This phase focuses on deployment and monitoring activities:

- AI packages the Unit into appropriate Deployment Units based on the application architecture and target environment.

- The team approves the deployment configuration and initiates rollout to target environments.

- AI analyzes application metrics, logs, and traces to identify anomalies and predict potential performance issues. Example: AI detects performance degradation during peak usage and proposes optimization strategies.

- AI integrates with operational procedures to suggest actions for system issues. When problems arise, AI recommends appropriate solutions based on the application\'s architecture and requirements.

- The team validates AI's recommendations, approves mitigations, and monitors resolution outcomes.

Once the Operations phase is complete, the team has successfully delivered a production-ready application using the complete AI-DLC methodology.
